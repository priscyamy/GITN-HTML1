MY TOP 5 FAVOURITE MOVIES

QUEEN AND SLIM

  • I love the movie "Queen & Slim" because it powerfully intertwines a gripping narrative with poignant social commentary. The film portrays the raw and emotional journey of two individuals who are thrust into a desperate situation, turning a routine traffic stop into a profound exploration of race, love, and justice. The chemistry between Daniel Kaluuya and Jodie Turner-Smith is electric, bringing depth to their characters' evolving relationship. Moreover, the cinematography beautifully captures the landscape of their journey, enhancing the storytelling with a blend of tension and tenderness. The film's ability to address critical societal issues while maintaining a deeply personal and human story resonates deeply with me

    • TITANIC

  • I love the movie "Titanic" because it masterfully blends historical drama with a timeless love story. James Cameron's direction brings the grandeur and tragedy of the Titanic's voyage to life with stunning visual effects and meticulous attention to detail. The romance between Jack and Rose, portrayed by Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet, is captivating and heartfelt, providing an emotional core that resonates deeply. The film's epic scale, combined with its intimate portrayal of personal stories amidst a monumental disaster, creates a moving and unforgettable cinematic experience

    • THE PURSUITS OF HAPPINESS

  • I love "The Pursuit of Happyness" because it is a deeply inspirational film that portrays the relentless spirit of human resilience and hope. Will Smith's portrayal of Chris Gardner, a man struggling against all odds to provide a better life for his son, is both moving and powerful. The film captures the essence of perseverance, highlighting how determination and hard work can lead to success despite overwhelming challenges. Its emotional depth and real-life basis make the story relatable and uplifting, reminding viewers of the importance of never giving up on their dreams
